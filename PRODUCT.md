# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Materials planners, supply-chain analysts, and project coordinators reviewing construction demand vs supply for Vietnamese localities. They open the dashboard during planning sessions, often with the source Google Sheet nearby, to find where shortages concentrate and whether surplus elsewhere can cover them.

## Product Purpose

Live demand–supply intelligence fed from Google Sheets (Appendix2 / DashData). Success means a planner can, in under a minute: set scope (period / project / material / location), see the shortage story, drill into a province or material, and verify numbers against the sheet — without scrolling through a wall of equal-weight charts.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, operational. The interface should feel like a trusted instrument, not a marketing report. Confidence comes from clear numbers, honest data-quality signals, and fast drill — not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Decorated “AI analytics” templates: glass panels, serif display numerals, numbered section chapters, side-stripe callouts, hero-metric KPI card grids
- Marketing dashboards with equal-weight chart museums
- Purple/glow SaaS aesthetics
- Landing-page motion choreography on filter changes

## Design Principles

1. **Task over template** — One primary surface for triage; deep analysis is a mode, not the default scroll.
2. **Scope is sacred** — Sticky filters and URL state always answer “what am I looking at?”
3. **Show the risk first** — Unmet, concentration, and outliers beat vanity totals.
4. **Earn every pixel** — No decorative glass, display fonts on data, or redundant demand→supply→unmet charts.
5. **Trust the sheet** — Integrity and reconciliation copy stay visible when totals diverge from the sheet summary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for body and controls. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for map fits, chart updates, and press feedback. Do not rely on color alone for shortage severity (pair with labels, ranks, and numeric values). Keyboard: period toggle and reset shortcuts; skip link to main content; error states keep a `main` landmark.
